## üìà Key Insights

### 1. Branch Performance
- Branches **A, B, and C** show relatively balanced sales performance.
- One branch slightly outperforms others, indicating potential best practices to replicate across locations.
- Branch-level benchmarking can help optimize operational strategies.

---

### 2. Product Line Analysis
Top-performing product lines by total sales include:
- **Food and beverages**
- **Sports and travel**
- **Home and lifestyle**
- **Electronic accessories**
- **Fashion accessories**

This highlights strong consumer demand in both essential and lifestyle product categories.

---

### 3. Tax and Gross Income Contribution
- **Tax (5%) accounts for ~4.55%** of total sales.
- **Net sales represent ~95.45%** of total.
- Gross income of **15.38K** reflects overall profitability and margin performance.

---

### 4. Payment Method Preferences
Customer payment distribution is well-balanced:
- **Ewallet**
- **Cash**
- **Credit Card**

No single payment method dominates, indicating the importance of supporting multiple payment options for customer convenience.

---

### 5. Customer Type Analysis
- **Members** contribute slightly more to total sales than **Normal** customers.
- Loyalty/membership programs appear to positively impact revenue.
- Opportunity exists to further grow member-based sales through targeted promotions.

---

### 6. Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales peak in **January**.
- A dip is observed in **February**.
- Sales recover in **March**.
- Seasonal and promotional effects may influence monthly performance.

---

### 7. Time-Based Sales Pattern
- Sales activity is spread consistently throughout the day.
- Higher transaction density is observed during mid-day and evening hours.
- This insight can help optimize staff scheduling and promotional timing.

---

### 8. Product Line Ratings
- Customer ratings are fairly distributed across product lines.
- No major underperforming category in terms of customer satisfaction.
- Indicates consistent product and service quality across departments.
